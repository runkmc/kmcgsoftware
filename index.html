Neos

Do you ever gaze into the night sky, thinking, "I wonder how many asteroids, massive bodies hurtling through space at mind-boggling speeds and capable of destroying all life should they strike Earth are dangerously close to us at this very moment?"

Wonder no more! Neos has got your back. Neos shows you all of the asteroids that are making their closest pass to Earth today. Be sure to check Neos every day to see which new group of giant space rocks could kill us all next time they pass by if their orbit is disturbed by a fraction of a percent. Earthquakr

Whenever I feel an earthquake, I immediately want to know the answers to two questions: Where was it centered, and how strong was it?

Earthquakr answers these questions for you.

Earthquakr uses your location to find nearby earthquakes and, using a totally complicated algorithm that someone much smarter than me came up with, displays only the earthquakes you would have felt at your location within the last 12 hours.

So the next time you hear your car alarm go off while your pets cower in fear; while you feel the earth beneath your feet, that same earth you once thought safe and sturdy, crumble and shatter; while you begin to question the trust you've placed in anything or anyone else, remember: at least now you have Earthquakr by your side.

Earthquakr. Whole lotta shaking going on down there. Contact me if you have any questions or concerns.
